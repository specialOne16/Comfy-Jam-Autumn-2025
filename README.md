<p>Serena frantically opens her front door, in search of her lost cat, Maple. The only problem is that Maple <em>was</em> inside...until she escaped out the door. Serena follows Maple's trail of paw prints into a pumpkin patch. She must find a way to get to her cat! Why on earth would Maple run outside in the first place?&nbsp;</p>
<p>Push pumpkins through the pumpkin patch and&nbsp;get your cat back home!</p>
